Data Analyst Salaries

A look at salaries across varying job titles of data professionals. Our dataset is from kaggle and contains information on salaries, the remote ratio, place of employment, amongst additional information.

To begin, select a job title below.

Job Title:

This plot shows the salaries reported by professionals by their Job Title. The plot uses the id number from our dataset on the x axis and the salaries associates on the y axis.

Our inspiration behind choosing this topic was from our curiosity about salaries of professionals in the field, as we are studying to become one of them.

Our interest is to see the different combinations in job titles, salary, and location that might indicate what opportunity to go after.
